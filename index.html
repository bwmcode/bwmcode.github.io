Abbas Khalili

I received my B.Sc. degree in electrical and computer engineering from University of Tehran, Iran, in 2016, and the M.Sc. degree in electrical and computer engineering from New York University (NYU) Tandon School of Engineering, USA, in 2018. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. I am also a member of NYU WIRELESS Center conducting research on next generation wireless networks. My research interests include wireless communications, low resolution ADCs, beam alignment, information theory, and machine learning.
